Product Requirements Document (PRD): SHOP.CO Storefront

1. Overview
SHOP.CO is a modern, mobile-first e-commerce storefront theme designed for a multi-tenant SaaS platform. The theme features a high-contrast, clean, and bold aesthetic with heavy typography and a streamlined user experience. This PRD covers the three core user flows provided: the Product Detail Page (PDP), the Product Listing Page (PLP/Category), and the Cart Page.

2. Global Elements

    Top Navigation (Mobile): Hamburger menu (left), Logo (center), Search icon, Cart icon, Profile icon (right) as seen in 1000043153.jpg.

    Top Navigation (Desktop): Logo, Category links (Shop, On Sale, New Arrivals, Brands), expanded Search bar, Cart icon, Profile icon (1000043152.jpg).

    Footer:

        Newsletter Block: Black rounded container with bold white text ("STAY UPTO DATE ABOUT OUR LATEST OFFERS"), an email input field, and a "Subscribe to Newsletter" primary button.

        Link Matrix: Company description, social links, and 4 columns of utility links (Company, Help, FAQ, Resources).

        Copyright & Payment Badges (Visa, Mastercard, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay).

3. Page Requirements

A. Product Detail Page (PDP)

    References: 1000043152.jpg (Desktop), 1000043153.jpg (Mobile).

    Breadcrumbs: Standard hierarchy (Home > Shop > Men > T-shirts).

    Image Gallery:

        Desktop: Large main image with a vertical column of thumbnails on the left.

        Mobile: Horizontal swipeable image carousel with pagination dots.

    Product Info: Title (Heavy Display Font), Star rating out of 5, Price block (Current Price, Strikethrough Original Price, Discount Badge), and a short text description.

    Selectors:

        Colors: Circular swatches with a checkmark indicator for the active state.

        Sizes: Pill-shaped toggle buttons (Small, Medium, Large, X-Large). Active state is black with white text.

    Action Row: Quantity selector (pill shape with -, number, +) alongside a primary "Add to Cart" button (black pill).

    Tabs: Product Details, Rating & Reviews, FAQs. The active tab has a solid bottom border.

    Reviews Section: Header with average rating, sorting dropdown, and "Write a Review" button. Review cards display star rating, Name with a verified green checkmark, review text, and post date. Includes a "Load More Reviews" button.

    Upsell: "YOU MIGHT ALSO LIKE" section featuring a 4-up (desktop) or 2-up (mobile) product grid.

B. Product Listing Page (PLP / Casual Category)

    References: 1000043154.jpg (Desktop), 1000043155.png & 1000043156.png (Mobile).

    Header: Category title ("Casual") with a product count and a "Sort by" dropdown. Mobile features a filter icon button next to the title.

    Sidebar Filters (Desktop): Accordion-style filters for Categories (T-shirts, Shorts, etc.), Price (range slider), Colors (swatch grid), Size (pill grid), and Dress Style. Includes an "Apply Filter" button.

    Product Grid:

        Cards feature a light gray background with rounded corners and no drop shadows.

        Layout: 3-column or 4-column on desktop, 2-column on mobile.

        Data: Product image, Title, Star Rating, Price (with discount pill if applicable).

    Pagination: Standard "Previous" / "Next" arrows with numbered pages at the bottom.

C. Cart Page

    References: 1000043157.jpg (Desktop), 1000043158.jpg (Mobile).

    Cart Items List:

        Displays product thumbnail, Title, Size, Color, Price.

        Includes a quantity adjuster (pill shape) and a red trash/delete icon aligned to the right.

    Order Summary Card:

        Line items for Subtotal, Discount (with red text indicating the minus amount), and Delivery Fee.

        Total price (bold).

        Promo Code input row with an "Apply" button.

        "Go to Checkout" primary CTA button with a right arrow icon.
